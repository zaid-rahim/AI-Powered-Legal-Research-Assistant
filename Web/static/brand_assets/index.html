Under Section 497 Cr.P.C, bail in non-bailable offences is at the court's discretion. The Session Court may grant bail if:

1. There is no reasonable ground to believe the accused committed the offence
2. The accused is a woman, child, or sick person (S.497(1) proviso)
3. Investigation is not completed within 30 days (S.497(2))

Courts weigh prima facie evidence, flight risk, and public interest per PLD 2021 SC 334.
