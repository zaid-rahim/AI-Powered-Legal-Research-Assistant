How it works

An AI that knows Pakistani law — deeply.

Step 1 — Sign in

Register a free account to unlock the chat interface. Your questions and past chats stay tied to your profile.

Step 2 — Ask a legal question

Type your question in English or Urdu. LawPakAI retrieves the most relevant judgments and statutes from a vetted Pakistani law corpus.

Step 3 — Read the cited answer

Every reply comes with source citations, a structured summary, and a knowledge-graph view that maps how cases relate.

Step 4 — Revisit past chats

All your conversations are saved on the server — log back in any time to reopen, search, and continue your research.

Under Section 497 Cr.P.C, bail in non-bailable offences is at the court's discretion. The Session Court may grant bail if:

1. There is no reasonable ground to believe the accused committed the offence
2. The accused is a woman, child, or sick person (S.497(1) proviso)
3. Investigation is not completed within 30 days (S.497(2))

Courts weigh prima facie evidence, flight risk, and public interest per PLD 2021 SC 334.
